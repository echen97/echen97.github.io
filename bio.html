The Biography of Ethan Chen

Early Life

Ethan Chen was born on July 31st, 1997 in New York City to Brian and Mabel Chen. He attended Metropolitan Montessori preschool on the Upper West side, where he was best friends with Liam Neeson's son Daniel Neeson. At the age of five he moved to Boston, Massachusetts where he and his family lived in a hotel and he attended Cambridge Montessori school. At age six he and his family moved to Weston, Massachusetts, where he attended the Country School of Weston. At age 9 him and his family moved to Battersea in London, where he attended Thomas's Battersea. After a year he moved back to Weston and went to The Meadowbrook school of Weston, before attending Weston High School and Tufts University.

Family

Ethan Has two siblings: a twin sister named Jacqueline who also goes to Tufts as well as a younger brother named Aidan. His parents, Mabel and Brian are still happily married. In addition, he has two : Champ, Black Lab, and Jade, a Golden Retriever. The rest of his extended family is spread out across the US and Asia.

Hobbies

Ethan has been playing tennis since the age of 6, and currently plays on the Tufts Men's Varsity Tennis Team. He played Cello from the age of four until the end of highschool. He sang acapella in the Weston Town Criers in High school, and sings Acapella in Tufts S-Factor.
